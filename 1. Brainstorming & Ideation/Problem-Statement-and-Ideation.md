# Brainstorming & Ideation

## Problem Statement

Many educational institutions still rely on manual library management systems that consume significant time and effort. Maintaining physical records often results in inaccurate information, duplicate entries, delayed updates, and difficulty in tracking issued books and fines. Manual systems also make report generation difficult and reduce operational efficiency.

## Existing System — Drawbacks

- Manual maintenance of book records using traditional ledgers
- Difficulty in tracking dynamically issued and returned books
- Increased paperwork and physical storage dependency
- Duplicate or incorrect data entries in handwritten logs
- Delayed fine calculation and complex tracking
- Difficulty compiling, sorting, and generating administrative reports
- Poor data security and vulnerability to physical damage
- Time-consuming manual search for book details
- No centralized database
- No automated notifications or field validations

## Idea

Build a **Smart Library Management System** on the ServiceNow low-code platform that digitizes and automates the entire library workflow — book management, member management, issue/return tracking, fine calculation, and reporting — with secure, role-based access.

## Scope

Applicable to schools, colleges, universities, public libraries, and corporate organizations. Designed to support both librarians and students with secure, role-appropriate access to library services. Extensible with barcode/QR scanning, email/SMS notifications, and online reservations.

## Advantages

- Reduces paperwork
- Improves operational efficiency
- Centralized data management
- Enhances data security via role-based access
- Reduces duplicate records
- Instant search for book and member records
